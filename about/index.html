About Us

Our Story

International Breakthrough Ministries was formed in 2010 by the Founder Pastor Souzie Balosa Lukeni who was born originally in Congo Kinshasa but moved to the UK to settle.

Pastor Melusi had worked very close to Pastor Souzie and when the woman of God passed away, God by his spirit led pastor Melusi to takeover. When he took over all there was nothing left as all the people had left after Pastor Souzie had passed on but God worked marvellously with Pastor Melusi to give him strength to rebuild.

Together with his wife Mrs Abegail Dube they have worked tirelessly to push the name of Jesus and to build the church. As we now speak International Breakthrough Ministries is in 3 cities across the UK and pastor Melusi is invited to speak all over the world.

Leadership

Pastor Melusi

Led by the Holy Spirit, Pastor Melusi has been instrumental in rebuilding International Breakthrough Ministries from the ground up. His passion for souls and dedication to the gospel has seen the ministry grow to three cities across the UK.

Pastor Melusi is known for his powerful preaching, deliverance ministry, and compassion for the lost. He travels internationally, spreading the good news and transforming lives through the power of Christ.

Mrs. Abegail Dube

Working alongside her husband, Mrs. Abegail Dube has been a pillar of strength and support in the ministry. Together, they have built a vibrant community of believers dedicated to advancing the kingdom of God.

Her heart for worship and prayer has created an atmosphere where God's presence is tangible, and lives are continually being transformed.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed by the power of the gospel, communities healed, and the name of Jesus exalted across the nations.

Our Mission

To preach the uncompromised word of God, demonstrate His love through practical charity work, and raise up disciples who will continue to spread the gospel to all nations.
