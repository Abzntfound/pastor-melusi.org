WHAT WE DO

Ministry Work

Community Fellowship

Pastor Melusi in IBM had worked very close and passionately delivering many from demon possession, healing the sick and many other deliverance work through the power of the holy spirit in Christ Jesus.

Our community fellowship gatherings are times of powerful worship, teaching from God's word, and experiencing His presence together. All are welcome to join us as we grow in faith and love.

Missionary Work

"…Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit"

Pastor Melusi is steering the leadership in several churches across the globe including South Africa and London. Impacting lives with the power and the love of Christ.

Our missionary work extends beyond borders, reaching nations with the transformative message of the gospel.

Charity Work

"…when Jesus landed and saw a large crowd, he had compassion on them and healed their sick"

Pastor Melusi has been reaching out to the less fortunate, prisoners and the needy for compassion. You are invited to support our charity initiatives.

We believe in demonstrating God's love through practical action, meeting both spiritual and physical needs in our communities.
